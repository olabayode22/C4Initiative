Sample Bingo Card

This is an example of what your personalized C4 Bingo card will look like. Each square represents a local Chelsea business. Click on any square to learn more about the business!

How to Play:

  1. Visit businesses on your card and make a purchase
  2. Keep your receipt and submit it through your account
  3. Mark squares on your card by submitting valid receipts
  4. Complete a winning pattern (line, four corners, X, or full card) to win prizes!

The center square is marked as FREE to help you complete patterns.
