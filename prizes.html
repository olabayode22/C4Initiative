Prizes & Rewards

Complete bingo patterns to win these exciting prizes from local Chelsea businesses.

How to Win

The C4 Bingo Program offers multiple ways to win prizes by completing different patterns on your bingo card.

Winning Patterns

Different bingo patterns qualify for different prize tiers. The more challenging the pattern, the more valuable the prize!

Single Line

FREE

Any horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line

Four Corners

FREE

All four corner squares

X Pattern

FREE

Both diagonal lines forming an X

Full Card

FREE

All 24 squares (excluding FREE)

Prize Categories

Prizes are organized into three tiers based on the pattern you complete.

Bronze Level

Line Bingo Prizes

  • $10 gift cards to participating businesses
  • 10-15% discounts on purchases
  • Free coffee or dessert with purchase
  • C4 Program merchandise (tote bags, pins)
  • Small product samples from local shops

Silver Level

Four Corners Prizes

  • $25 gift cards to participating businesses
  • 20-25% discounts on purchases
  • Free appetizer or side dish with meal
  • Premium C4 Program merchandise (t-shirts, water bottles)
  • Medium-value products from local shops
  • 2-for-1 service offers

Gold Level

Full Card Prizes

  • $50-100 gift cards to participating businesses
  • 30-50% discounts on purchases
  • Free meals or services
  • Exclusive C4 Program collector items
  • High-value products from local shops
  • Entry into quarterly grand prize drawings
  • VIP access to special events
