How C4 Bingo Works

The Chelsea Cannabis Community Connection (C4) Bingo Program is designed to promote local businesses in Chelsea while providing a fun, interactive experience for participants. Here's everything you need to know about how the program works.

Program Overview

C4 Bingo is a digital bingo game that encourages participants to visit multiple local businesses in Chelsea. Each square on your bingo card represents a different participating business. When you visit a business and make a purchase, you can submit your receipt to mark that square on your card. Complete a bingo pattern to win prizes!

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Register for an Account

Create your free C4 Bingo account to get started. You'll need to provide your name, email address, and create a password. Once registered, you'll have access to your personalized bingo card.

Register Now

Step 2: Explore Your Bingo Card

After registering, you'll receive a digital bingo card with a 5x5 grid. Each square (except the FREE space in the center) represents a participating local business in Chelsea. You can view details about each business by clicking on their square.

View Sample Card

Step 3: Visit Local Businesses

Visit the businesses on your bingo card. You can browse the complete directory of participating businesses to plan your visits. Each business offers unique products or services, and many provide special promotions for C4 Bingo participants.

Browse Businesses

Step 4: Make a Purchase & Keep Your Receipt

When you visit a participating business, make a purchase and be sure to keep your receipt. You'll need this receipt to validate your visit and mark the corresponding square on your bingo card.

Step 5: Submit Your Receipt

After your visit, log into your C4 Bingo account and submit your receipt information. You can either upload a photo of your receipt or enter the receipt details manually. Our system will validate your submission and mark the appropriate square on your card.

See Receipt Submission

Step 6: Complete a Bingo Pattern

Continue visiting businesses and submitting receipts to mark squares on your card. When you complete a valid bingo pattern (horizontal, vertical, diagonal line, or full card), you'll automatically be notified that you've won!

Step 7: Claim Your Prize

Once you've completed a bingo, you can claim your prize through the website. Different patterns and achievements qualify for different prize tiers. You'll receive instructions on how to redeem your specific prize.

View Prize Catalog
